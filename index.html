CAMERON, Christopher JF

Postdoctoral Associate, Yale University
Chicago, IL

I am a computational biologist working as a Postdoctoral Associate at Yale University (New Haven, CT, USA) under the supervision of Drs. Mark Gerstein and Yong Xiong. My postdoctoral work focuses on exploring the intersection of Cryo-EM and genomics. Under the supervision of Drs. Mathieu Blanchette and Josée Dostie at McGill University (Montréal, QC, Canada), I obtained a PhD in Computer Science with a concentration in Bioinformatics (2019). My PhD dissertation focused on high resolution bioinformatics approaches, statistical modeling, and machine learning in 3D regulatory genomics – in particular, data obtained from Chromosome Conformation Capture (3C) and Chromatin ImmunoPrecipitation (ChIP).

If you are interested in collaborating with me, please contact me as I am always happy to talk science over a beer.
